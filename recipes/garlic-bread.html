Garlic Bread Recipe

Description

"This is a story, a fairytale if you will, of three of the greatest foods in the world coming together. Butter, garlic, and bread... We combine them together to make something greater than the sum of its parts."

- Joshua Weissman

Reference

Perfect Homemade Garlic Bread [2:16~3:55]

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of Unsalted Butter (220g)
  • 4-6 Cloves of Garlic
  • 1/4 Cup of Parsley (Italian Flat Leaf)
  • 1/4 Cup of Parmesan Oregano (Cheese)
  • Bread (Chibatta Loaf recommended)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit (205 degrees Celsius) or your airfryer to similar temperatures.
  2. Soften your Butter in the microwave (20-40 seconds).
  3. Finely chop Garlic. Mix Garlic and Butter into a bowl.
  4. Add your finely chopped Parsley into the mix.
  5. Grate your Parmesan Oregano into the mixing bowl.
  6. Mix everything together until the mixture is evenly spread.
  7. Cut your Bread in half to maximize surface area (for maximum spread) and spread the Garlic Butter Mixture onto the nicely cut bread.
  8. Place your Buttered Up Bread onto a baking sheet and place that into the oven for 10-12 minutes (For airfryer, it may take longer, look for a toasted golden brown finish).
  9. Slice the bread into 1x1" chunky pieces.
  10. Test your creation and add seasoning as needed. Enjoy!
