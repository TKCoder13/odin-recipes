Garlic Bread Croutons Recipe

Ingredients

  • 6 Cloves of Garlic
  • Kosher Salt
  • 3 Anchovie Filets Packed in Olive Oil
  • 2 Large Egg Yolks
  • 1 & 1/2 Lemons
  • 1 Large Tsp of Dijon Mustard (18g)
  • Oil of Your Choice (Vegetable Oil, Avocado Oil, etc.)
  • Parmaesan Oregano
  • Boston/Bib Lettuce (Preference of Cook)
  • Romaine Lettuce

Steps

  1. Dice and smear Garlic Cloves & Anchovie Filets until it has a paste consistency.
  2. Add the Egg Yolk, Dijon Mustard, and Garlic Anchovie Paste to a large mixing bowl and mix.
  3. Slowly drip oil into the mixture while whisking until it becomes a viscious paste.
  4. Once done, finely grate the Parmaesan Oregano into the mixture. Whisk it into the mixture.
  5. Wash and prepare all your Lettuce.
  6. Grab your Croutons (or make them if not prepared yet) and toss them into bowl with Lettuce and the Ceasar Sauce.
  7. Taste your product and add salt or other seasonings to your liking. Top it off with some Grated Cheese.
